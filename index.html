Materials Accepted

Ferrous Metals

  • Steel
  • Cast Iron
  • Wrought Iron
  • Heavy Melting Steel

Non-Ferrous Metals

  • Copper (Wires, Pipes)
  • Aluminum (Cans, Extrusions, Alloys)
  • Brass (Fittings, Rods)
  • Bronze
  • Lead

E-Waste / Other

  • Electric Motors
  • Insulated Wires
  • Catalytic Converters
  • Car Batteries (Lead-Acid)
  • Electronic Scrap (Limited Types)

Materials NOT Accepted

  • Hazardous Waste (Chemicals, Asbestos)
  • Tires
  • Pressurized Tanks (Propane, Oxygen)
  • Flammable Materials
  • Radioactive Materials
  • Fluorescent Light Bulbs
